# GhostPay: 4-Minute Demo Walkthrough Script
**Final Submission Video:** https://youtu.be/hVdVOANnKoM


Follow this guide to record a high-scoring submission video!

## 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook & Vision
-   **Show:** The Landing Page.
-   **Say:** "Welcome to GhostPay, the privacy-first institutional payroll protocol built for the iExec Vibe Challenge. We solve the problem of public salary exposure by bridging iExec Nox confidential vaults with a professional institutional dashboard."

## 0:30 - 1:30 | Setup & Wrapping (Employer)
-   **Show:** Employer Dashboard.
-   **Action:** Connect Wallet -> Enter 5,000 in Wrap box -> Click **Execute Deposit**.
-   **Say:** "As an employer, I can wrap standard USDC into confidential cUSDC. This moves funds into the Nox enclave, where they are shielded from public view while remaining fully liquid for my payroll needs."

## 1:30 - 2:30 | Batch Dispatch (Innovation)
-   **Show:** Confidential Contacts Page.
-   **Action:** Select 2-3 contacts -> Click **Dispatch to Selected** -> Click **Execute Confidential Payout**.
-   **Say:** "Managing a global team is seamless. I can select contacts from my private address book and execute a bulk confidential batch. On-chain observers see one transaction, but individual recipient amounts are end-to-end encrypted via iExec Nox."

## 2:30 - 3:30 | AI Audit & Identity
-   **Show:** GhostPay AI Assistant & Settings.
-   **Action:** Type "What is the total protocol volume?" in the chat -> Show the **Shadow Mode** toggle.
-   **Say:** "We've integrated ChainGPT to provide a confidential audit experience. Employers get real-time insights into burn rates and volume without compromising encryption. We also feature Shadow Mode for secure reporting in public spaces."

## 3:30 - 4:00 | The Claim & Conclusion
-   **Show:** Employee View.
-   **Action:** Toggle to Employee -> Show 3000 cUSDC balance -> Click **Withdraw**.
-   **Say:** "Employees receive their salaries privately. They can claim their funds with one click, unwrapping them back to standard USDC. GhostPay is the ultimate infrastructure for a private, professional Web3 workforce. Built with iExec Nox and ChainGPT."
